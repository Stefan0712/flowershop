Services Portfolio

Custom Flower Arrangements

Our skilled florists will work with you to create a unique and beautiful floral arrangement for any occasion, whether it's a birthday, wedding, or corporate event. We'll take into account your personal style and preferences to ensure that the end result is exactly what you envisioned.

Prices vary depending on the size and complexity of the arrangement, starting at $50.

Custom Garden Creation

Want to transform your outdoor space into a beautiful and functional garden? Our team of experts will work with you to design and install a custom garden that meets your specific needs and preferences. Whether you're looking for a vegetable garden, a flower garden, or a combination of both, we've got you covered.

Prices vary depending on the size and complexity of the garden, starting at $500.

Plant Care and Maintenance

Keep your plants healthy and thriving with our plant care and maintenance services. We'll visit your home or business on a regular basis to water, prune, fertilize, and otherwise care for your plants, ensuring that they stay healthy and beautiful all year round.

Prices vary depending on the number and type of plants, starting at $25 per visit.

Gift Baskets and Floral Arrangements

Looking for the perfect gift for a friend, family member, or colleague? We offer a wide variety of gift baskets and floral arrangements that are sure to delight anyone. From fresh-cut flowers to gourmet snacks, we've got something for everyone.

Prices vary depending on the contents of the basket or arrangement, starting at $35.

Landscape Maintenance

Keep your lawn and outdoor space looking beautiful with our landscape maintenance services. We'll mow your lawn, trim your hedges, and keep your outdoor space looking tidy and well-maintained all year round.

Prices vary depending on the size and complexity of the space, starting at $50 per visit.
